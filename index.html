About

Adonis is a Lebanese pop-rock band renowned for its signature Arabic lyrics and blistering live performances.
The four-piece was formed in 2011 by Anthony Adonis (vocals, keys) and Joey Abou Jawdeh (guitar). he pair, who met during their architecture studies, were later joined by Nicola Hakim on drums and Gio Fikany on bass guitar.
